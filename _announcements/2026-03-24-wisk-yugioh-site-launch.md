After years of writing, competing and documenting, we've built a permanent home for the Slovenian Yu-Gi-Oh! community.

**Wisk Yu-Gi-Oh!** brings together the full archive of our community's writing — from the earliest tournament records in 2010 all the way to modern Goat Format strategy guides.

---

## Why this matters

Slovenia has a small but passionate Yu-Gi-Oh! community with over two decades of competitive history. Over the years, players wrote deck analyses, guides, interviews and tournament reports — content scattered across forums, Facebook groups and private archives that was at risk of disappearing entirely.

**This site is our collective memory.**

---

## What you'll find here

### 📚 Slovenian articles

Over 140 articles in Slovenian covering everything from early deck profiles in 2013 to in-depth modern Goat Format strategy. Written by community members who shaped how Yu-Gi-Oh! was understood and played in Slovenia.

### 🏆 Tournament reports

Coverage going back to 2010 — local tournaments, the Slovenian national championship, and international events. A documented competitive history of Slovenian Yu-Gi-Oh! in one place.

### ✍️ Modern writing

Alongside the archive, we continue to publish new articles and analysis. Goat Format is alive, and we're still writing about it.

### 🌍 English section

For the international community: English articles and tournament reports covering Goat Format from a Slovenian perspective.

## Built by the community

This archive wouldn't exist without every person who took the time to write an article, file a tournament report, or share their knowledge. To every author who ever contributed — **this site is yours.**
